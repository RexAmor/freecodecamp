Biography

  • 1935 - Born in the US
  • 1950 - Fights in the Korean War
  • 1961 - Carries out covert operations duing the Vietnam War
  • 1964 - Takes part in Operation Snake Eater, awarded Distinguished Service Cross by the U.S. Presisdent, receives the nickname "Big Boss"
  • 1970 - Arrested and falsely charged with treason by the Department of Defense. He escapes and clears his name right after that after infiltrating and compromising FOX, a CIA's special force. He is left in charge of FOXHOUND, to carry on the tradition of FOX.
  • 1971 - Revered as the perfect soldier, Big Boss is cloned by Zero, his former colleagues. The clones are named Solid Snake, Liquid Snake, and Solidus Snake. Big Boss leaves the US, fights in various fronts, gathers funds and influence to find a way to oppose Zero.
